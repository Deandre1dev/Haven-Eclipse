About us

Haven Eclipse a band formed by 3 amazing Metal artist Max, Joey and Ben.The band was formed on the 11 July 2017
Just recently after they have met at tryouts at an audition for a gig in Chicago.Haven Eclipse have honed a technical, multi-genre
approach to Metal music that sounds like the product of a completely diffrent genre.Ripping, Serpantine riffs and bashing
breakdowns will make it almost impossible for Haven Eclipse to lose your attention.They have recieved an a award
for the best Metal group band in 2018 awarded ny the New York Times.This band has just entered the
music scene and they are already making waves.
